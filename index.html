About

You could say it was a foreshadowing of things to come, as Lemuel tinkered with website templates and content creation in his early teenage years. He was fascinated with the creative control he had when it came to web development, the colors he could add, the layouts he conceived in his mind and the visual appeal of a complete webpage. He would later revisit this field of work in his mid-twenties, this time not as a past-time but as a career goal. Though a novice, he retains the fascination he had in his early years and has developed a newfound sense of hunger to learn as he moves inch by inch toward his goal.
